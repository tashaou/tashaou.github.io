About Me

Xinlin is currently a student at Massachusetts College of Art and Design, she is dual majoring in Industrial design and Ceramic. While she loves to craft beautiful and functional products, she also loves to spend time to build ceramics, her ceramic work mostly focused on mugs, bowls, and plates, while some are abstract pieces.
